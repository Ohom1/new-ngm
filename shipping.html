Course Delivery Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2024

1. Course Access Delivery

As we provide online courses, our delivery process is digital:

  • Instant access upon successful payment
  • Access credentials sent via email
  • 24/7 availability of course content
  • No physical materials shipped

2. Access Timeline

After purchase:

  • Login credentials: Within 10 minutes
  • Course materials: Immediate access
  • Support resources: Available instantly
  • Course updates: Automatic delivery

3. Technical Requirements

To ensure smooth course delivery, you need:

  • Stable internet connection (minimum 1 Mbps)
  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • PDF reader for course materials
  • Required software as per course specifications

4. Course Material Format

Our courses are delivered in various formats:

  • Interactive learning materials
  • Downloadable PDFs
  • Interactive quizzes
  • Project files and resources

5. Access Duration

Course access is provided as follows:

  • Standard courses: 12 months access
  • Specialization programs: 18 months access
  • Lifetime access courses: Unlimited duration
  • Extension options available if needed

6. Download Limitations

To protect our intellectual property:

  • Course content: Online access
  • PDFs: Downloadable with watermark
  • Resources: Limited downloads
  • Practice files: Unlimited access
