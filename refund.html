Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: December 12, 2024

1. Course Cancellation

We understand that circumstances may arise requiring you to cancel your course enrollment. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair and transparent:

  • Cancellation within 24 hours of purchase: Full refund
  • Cancellation within 7 days of purchase: 80% refund
  • Cancellation after accessing course content: No refund

2. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at refunds@ngmtechgrowth.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  3. Our team will review your request within 48 hours
  4. If approved, refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are eligible under the following conditions:

  • Course content significantly differs from description
  • Technical issues prevent course access
  • Request made within the specified timeframe
  • No significant course content has been accessed

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refund:

  • Downloaded course materials
  • Completed assignments or projects
  • Courses accessed beyond 7 days
  • Additional services or add-ons

5. Payment Refund Method

Refunds will be processed through the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Card: 5-7 business days
  • UPI: 2-3 business days
  • Net Banking: 3-5 business days

6. Course Access After Cancellation

Upon cancellation:

  • Course access will be immediately revoked
  • Downloaded materials should be deleted
  • Certificates will be invalidated

7. Special Circumstances

We may consider refunds outside the standard policy in exceptional cases:

  • Medical emergencies (with documentation)
  • Natural disasters
  • Technical failures on our part
